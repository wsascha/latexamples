For \(s \in \mathbb{R} \setminus 0 \) we get
\begin{align*}
    `*(1-s) + s`*(1-s) + s^2`*(1-s) + \ldots     & = 1             \\
    1 + s + s^2 + \ldots = \sum_{n=0}^\infty s^n & = \frac{1}{1-s}
\end{align*}
such that
\begin{equation}
    \sum_{n=0}^\infty 2^n = -\frac{1}{2},
\end{equation}
if \(s = 2\).
